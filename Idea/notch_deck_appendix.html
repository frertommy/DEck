LEAGUES
Why off-season matters

The off-season is when fundamentals get rewritten. Closing markets concentrates months of information into a single violent reopen gap. We stay live.

Transfers + Free Agency
The single biggest driver of team strength. A confirmed star signing reprices the entire team baseline — and it happens in the off-season.
Manager + Staff Changes
A sacking or hire changes win probability, playing style, and market sentiment overnight. Overwhelmingly an off-season event.
Injury Returns + Surgery
Star player availability is a first-order pricing input. Rehab timelines and preseason fitness — tradeable signals that only exist between seasons.

A7 · Legal Wedge
A perpetual derivative. Not an event contract.

No settlement, no expiry, no single-game payoff. Notch is a continuous index derivative — designed for the federal derivative framework from day one.

No settlement. No expiry. No game to bet on.
Positions stay open across matches, transfers, managerial changes, and entire off-seasons. There is no cash-out event tied to a game result. Price moves continuously — like an equity, not a bet.
Continuous index, not an event contract
Team strength index synthesizes bookmaker odds, match history, transfers, injuries, and network effects into a forward-looking estimate. No single match determines the price — the same way no single earnings report determines the S&P.
Perp infrastructure already CFTC-approved
CFTC greenlit crypto perpetuals on Coinbase Derivatives, July 2025. SEC + CFTC "Project Crypto" summit, Jan 2026 — onshoring perpetual derivatives is an explicit regulatory priority.
Designed for the federal derivative framework
Standard derivatives mechanics — margin, funding rate, liquidation — that courts and regulators already recognize. Three-entity structure isolates pricing, clearing, and distribution.
